What happens if we look at educational attainment associated with rates of fitness with a direct corrolation of obesity?



The results show that education and fitness have a direct corrolation with the rate of obesity in local proximity.

The more educated a group of people is, the more likely they will exercise and take care of their body.

And of course, the more a group of people exercises, the less likely it is that the group is overweight.
